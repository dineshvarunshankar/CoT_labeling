# tripod

## Human Findings

### 001 - Tripod posture combines forward lean and arm bracing

A yes answer should be supported by the subject leaning forward with one or both
arms braced on knees, thighs, ground, or another surface for upper-body support.

### 002 - Ordinary sitting or crawling is not tripod

Do not label tripod when the subject is simply sitting, kneeling, crawling,
lying, or using hands for balance without a clear forward-leaning braced posture.

### 003 - Bracing location should be spatially described

The CoT should state where the hands or arms are braced relative to the torso,
knees, ground, or object, and why that geometry does or does not match tripod.
